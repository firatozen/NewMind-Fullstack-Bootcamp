Creme Brulee Recipe

The perfect dessert with a creamy, velvety texture and a caramelized top.

Ingredients

  • 400 ml cream
  • 100 ml milk
  • 5 egg yolks
  • 60 grams sugar
  • 2 ml vanilla extract
  • For Topping: 40 grams sugar

Chef's Tips

Cool the dessert, sprinkle a layer of sugar, and caramelize with a torch for the perfect crispy top.

Instructions

  1. Heat the milk and cream until boiling.
  2. In a bowl, beat egg yolks, vanilla, and sugar until the sugar dissolves.
  3. Gradually mix the hot milk into the eggs, stirring continuously.
  4. Strain the mixture twice, then let it rest for 10 minutes to settle the foam.
  5. Pour into ramekins, place in a water bath, and bake at 125°C for 35-40 minutes.
  6. Cool, refrigerate, sprinkle with sugar, and caramelize the top with a torch.
